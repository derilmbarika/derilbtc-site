About DerilBTC

DerilBTC started in 2018 in Buea, Cameroon’s Silicon Mountain, with a simple belief: Cameroonians deserve a safe, honest way to move their money.

Back then, buying crypto here meant trusting strangers and hoping. Too many people got burned. We built on the opposite approach: pay every time, publish real rates, document every trade, and treat every client like a long-term relationship. Because that’s what they become.

Eight years later, more than 300 clients trust DerilBTC with their transactions: students, traders, importers, freelancers, and families. Most came the same way, through a friend who said “use these people, they’re solid.”

Today we’ve grown beyond Bitcoin. We pay school fees in Canada and the USA, book international flights, and help importers pay suppliers across the world. Tomorrow, we’re building toward Cameroon’s most trusted exchange platform. But the promise stays exactly what it was on day one:

Your money arrives. Every time.
