What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin (BTC) is digital money you fully own and can send anywhere in the world, without a bank’s permission. Its price moves with the market, which is why some people buy it to hold and others to send value fast.

What is USDT?

USDT (Tether) holds the value of the US dollar - 1 USDT stays worth about 1 USD. Cameroonians use it to save in dollars, pay suppliers, and receive money from abroad without watching the value swing.

What is a wallet?

A wallet is a free app that holds your coin and gives you an address to receive it. Don’t have one? Tell us - we’ll help you set up and secure a wallet at no cost before your first buy.

How do I pay?

MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money or bank transfer. You see the full XAF total before paying, send it, and your coin arrives. Simple, and we walk first-timers through every step.
